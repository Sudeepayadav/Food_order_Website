Purpose


Food provides essential nutrients necessary for the body's growth, maintenance, and overall health. These nutrients include carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water.Food plays a significant role in cultures around the world, often serving as a means of celebration, socialization, and identity. Traditional dishes and cooking methods vary widely across different regions and ethnicities.



Nutrition


Fruits and Vegetables: Rich sources of vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber. Grains: Including wheat, rice, oats, and barley, providing carbohydrates and fiber. Protein Sources: Such as meat, poultry, fish, eggs, legumes, and nuts, essential for building and repairing tissues. Dairy Products: Milk, cheese, and yogurt, supplying calcium, protein, and other nutrients. Fats and Oils: Butter, olive oil, and other sources of healthy fats necessary for energy and nutrient absorption. Processed Foods: Ready-to-eat meals, snacks, and beverages that undergo manufacturing processes, often containing added sugars, salt.
